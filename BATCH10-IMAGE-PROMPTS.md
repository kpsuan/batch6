# Q1, Q30 Image Generation Prompts for Gemini

This document contains all options from Q1 (Section 0: Welcome) and Q30 (Section 7: Recommendation, Batch 10 — the platform-entry / platform-exit bookend pair) with their "How This Sounds" descriptions and corresponding Gemini image generation prompts.

## IMPORTANT: Diversity and Photography Requirements

**For ALL prompts below, append the following instructions:**

> "Shot on Kodak Portra 400 or Fujifilm Pro 400H film. Photorealistic, not illustration or cartoon. Real human subjects. Warm film grain, natural skin tones.
>
> **Rotate diversity across images:**
> - Ethnicities: Filipino, Indian, Nigerian, American, British, Chinese, Southeast Asian (Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian), Latino, Middle Eastern
> - **Occupations:** Vary widely — teachers, retired factory workers, shopkeepers, taxi drivers, tailors, accountants, grandparents, construction workers, bakers, hairdressers, librarians, farmers, engineers, cleaners, artists, truck drivers, small-business owners, social workers, chefs, translators. Healthcare workers appear where the narrative explicitly requires one (Q1 and Q30 are HCW-centered, so HCW imagery is appropriate here).
> - Include same-sex couples (two men or two women) in 20-30% of couple/family scenes
> - Include people with visible disabilities (wheelchair users, prosthetic limbs, mobility aids, hearing aids, visual impairment canes) in 15-20% of images
> - Vary ages, body types, and backgrounds"

## Q1: Welcome to A Whole Family Matter
**Question:** Is A Whole Family Matter for you?

**Question Title Image Prompt:** Create a threshold photorealistic photograph of a Filipina nurse in her 40s, scrubs still on after a thirteen-hour shift, standing in the doorway of her own kitchen with her hand resting on the frame. She has charted end-of-life wishes for forty patients today in two languages. The platform is open on her phone in her other hand, asking if A Whole Family Matter is for her. The doorway is the threshold between her work-self and her own self. Kitchen evening light on a hand on a doorframe and a face deciding whether to step through. Shot on Kodak Portra 400 film. Style: documentary photography, threshold, beginning, showing that asking whether a platform is for you begins with stepping out of the role you carry for others.
